Lasagna recipe

Description

Homemade lasagna is the best!

The dish consist of meat sauce and cheese sauce which is layered with pasta plates

Ingredients

    9 pasta plates

    3 dl shredded cheese for the top

    Meat sauce

  • 150 g bacon or pancetta
  • 2 ss neutral oil
  • 400 g minced meat
  • 1 thinly sliced garlic
  • 1 nicely chopped carrot
  • 1 nicely chopped cellery
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 2 tbsp tomate puré
  • 2 cans of hermetic tomatoes (á 400 g)
  • 2 dl meat broth
  • 2 tea spoons dried oregano

    • Cheese sauce

  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 3 tbsp flour
  • 6 dl milk
  • 5 tbsp shredded parmegano
  • 1 tea spoon salt
  • 0.5 tea spoon of pepper
  • 0,5 tea spoon nutmeg
