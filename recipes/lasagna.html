Lasagna recipe

Description

Homemade lasagna is the best!

The dish consist of meat sauce and cheese sauce which is layered with pasta plates

Ingredients

    9 pasta plates

    3 dl shredded cheese for the top

    Meat sauce

  • 150 g bacon or pancetta
  • 2 ss neutral oil
  • 400 g minced meat
  • 1 thinly sliced garlic
  • 1 nicely chopped carrot
  • 1 nicely chopped cellery
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 2 tbsp tomate puré
  • 2 cans of hermetic tomatoes (á 400 g)
  • 2 dl meat broth
  • 2 tea spoons dried oregano

    • Cheese sauce

  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 3 tbsp flour
  • 6 dl milk
  • 5 tbsp shredded parmegano
  • 1 tea spoon salt
  • 0.5 tea spoon of pepper
  • 0,5 tea spoon nutmeg

Steps

  1. Cut the bacon in small cubes. Put oil in a hot pan and fry the bacon until its golden. Put in some more oil and brown the meat.
  2. Put in onion, carrot and cellery until its soft and shiny. Put in the bacon and meat.
  3. Put in the puré and let it cook for a couple of minutes. Put in tomatoes, broth and spices. Let the sauce cook for atleast 10 minutes.
  4. Melt butter in a pot and stir in flour. Put milk in it while you stir. Let it boil for aprox. 10 minutes. Put in parmegano and let the cheese melt.
  5. Put meat sauce, pasta plates and cheese sauce layered in a form. Finish with pasta plates and cheese sauce. Sprinkle shredded cheese.
  6. Cook it for 30-40 minutes on 200 degrees Celsius.
