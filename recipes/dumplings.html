Dumplings

Description

Dumplings are filled wheat dough pillows, and they come in all varieties!

In this recipe the dumplings will be filled with pork, chili, garlic, soy sauce and sesame sauce

Ingredients

    Filling

  • 400 g pork
  • 2 spring onions
  • 1 nicely chopped chili
  • 2 boats shredded garlic
  • 50 g bamboo shoots
  • 2 ts soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame sauce

    • Dip Sauce

  • 8 ts hoisin sauce
  • 2 ts sweet soy sauce
  • 1 ts sesame seeds
  • Juice from 1 lime

    • Dough

  • 5 dl wheat flour
  • 2 dl water
  • 2 ts wheat flour for rolling out your dough

Steps

  1. Cut the carrots and spring onions into thin slices. Shred heart salat. Store the vegetables in a cold place until serving.
  2. Stir hoisin sauce, sweet soy sauce, sesame seeds and lime juice to a Dip Sauce. Put it in the fridge until the dumplings are done.
  3. Mix pork, spring onions, chili, garlic, bamboo shoots, soy sauce and sesame oil in a bowl.
  4. Put flour and water quickly together in a food processor. The mixture will look lumpy, but it will gather and feel agile if you squeeze it. Throw the mixture out on a bench, and squeeze it together to a dough.
  5. Cut 1/3 of the dough off. Wrap the rest of the dough in plastic. Put some flour on your work bench and roll the dough to aprox 2 mm thick.
  6. Use a glass or something with a diameter of 6-8 cm to make circles and punch it through the dough.
  7. Fill the dumplings with a tea spoon of the filling. Use some water along 180 degrees of the edge of the circle. Fold the dough together, make 5-6 folds in a pattern along the edge.
  8. When all the dumplings are folded you're ready to fry them. Put oil in a hot pan. Throw all the dumplings in there, and brown them one medium heat on one side. Put 1 dl of water in the pan, and put a lid on. Boil them until all the water has vaporized. Cook them for 2 more minutes.

    9. Throw them in your mouth hole. Enjoy.
